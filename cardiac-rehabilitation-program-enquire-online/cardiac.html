What is "EECP" Treatment?
EECP(Enhanced External Counter Pulsation) Treatment is an US FDA approved non-invasive heart treatment. It increases the blood flow to the heart and opens new blood vessels, called collaterals. EECP treatment at Narayana Health is supervised by an Internationally accredited Cardiologist with more than 24 years of experience . The treatment is controlled by certified EECP technicians and is monitored using dedicated heart monitors.It is beneficial for patients with heart disease, diabetes, kidney disease, ischemic stroke, ED, low energy, low stamina, sub-optimal physical performance and many other medical conditions.
Benifits Of EECP Treatment:

Creates new blood vessels:

Alternate pathways or (collaterals), which open new channels for blood flow around the blocked arteries in the heart, are formed by ECP treatment. Thus, it creates natural bypasses in the heart.

Leads to a stronger Heart:

It increases blood flow to the heart, which enriches the blood with oxygen, strengthens the heart muscles and reduces the stiffness of arteries.

Reduces angina:

It reduces or eliminates chest pain (angina) and improves cardio-vascular fitness.

Improves quality of life:

It improves stamina, alertness and energy levels, and reduces the heart work load.

Enhances blood circulation:

It improves blood circulation in the heart and to the body, including organs like lungs, kidneys, brain, liver, etc.

Improves organ function:

It increases the flow of oxygen and nutrients, increases the heart output and the functioning of all the body organs.

Who can undergo EECP treatment?
  • Those who have heart blockage or heart disease
  • Patients who have chest pain or angina (despite having undergone surgery) and want a possible reduction in their medications.
  • Patients who are not suitable for bypass surgery and angioplasty.
  • People with the history of heart attack
  • People suffering from systemic and metabolic dysfunctions that affect blood flow.
Mechanism of EECP Treatment:

EECP heart treatment is a non-surgical and pain-free procedure. As the patient lies down, 3 ECG electrodes are applied to the chest (pulse wave meter) and are connected to an EECP machine. Pressure cuffs are wrapped around the calves, thighs and buttocks.

Inflation of cuffs in a sequential manner

Enhances blood flow to the heart

Rapid deflation of cuffs

Enhances overall blood circulation
